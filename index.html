OUR VISION

Dream En Visualize,We help you actualize

The vision of MESA KU is to be an active problem solving organization through innovation and design in mechanical engineering and negotiating corporate partnerships with other engineering companies

OUR MISSION

Board And journey with us

The mission of this association is to mobilize people of goodwill to support mechanical engineering students in our obsessive quest to enhance and improve the career standards of Kenyatta University This shall in the long run, build better ties between our school and the key players in the corporate world as we strive to strike majestic heights in the engineering and academic quarters

OBJECTIVES

Our Aims And Goals

  1. Creating a work branch where students can be trained on various design tools including CAD, CAM, MATLAB, AutoCAD mechanical, Solid Works and the Inventor
  2. Expose students to real design work by finding engineering solutions to various problems facing the immediate community This will be achieved by identifying the challenges and device methods of curbing the problems facing the engineering world
  3. To update the students with the ever changing technology in the engineering world This will be achieved by seminars, conferences, etc
  4. Participating in various engineering exhibition including Robotics competition, Engineering Expo and Engineering innovation and design
  5. To carry out research on skills and our manipulation competence in machine process, fabrication, analyzing and to strive as a corporate and industrial world of mechanical engineering though field trips
  6. To enable the engineering student members to strengthen their suitability for professional employment and marketability in engineering related fields
